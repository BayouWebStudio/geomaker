Privacy Policy · Updated 2026-07-06

GeoMaker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking. The app makes no network requests — every pattern is generated on your device.

The details

  • No data collection. GeoMaker gathers no personal information, usage analytics, identifiers, or location. There is nothing to opt out of, because nothing is collected.
  • No network access. The app is fully self-contained and works offline. It does not contact any server, including ours.
  • Your settings stay on your device. Your last session — pattern, seed, palette, slider values, favorites — is saved locally using standard on-device storage so the app reopens where you left off. It never leaves the device.
  • Images you create stay yours. Saving hands the image to the iOS share sheet so you choose where it goes. GeoMaker does not upload or retain a copy. Saving to your photo library uses the standard iOS permission and only happens when you choose it.
  • No tracking. No Advertising Identifier (IDFA), no App Tracking Transparency prompts, no cross-app tracking.
  • Purchases are handled by Apple. The optional one-time unlock is processed entirely by the App Store (StoreKit). GeoMaker never sees your payment details — it only receives Apple's yes/no answer about whether the unlock is owned, checked on your device.
  • Children. GeoMaker is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

App Privacy label

In App Store Connect this app is declared as Data Not Collected.
