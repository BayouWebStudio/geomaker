GEOMAKER

a generative-art pattern studio

Shape organic, geometric, fractal and traditional patterns with live controls — every piece generated entirely on your device, then saved or shared whenever you like.

Privacy

GeoMaker collects no data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads and no tracking, and the app makes no network requests — everything runs offline on your device. Your last settings are stored locally so the app reopens where you left off. Images are only saved or shared when you choose to.
