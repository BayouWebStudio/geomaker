# GeoMaker — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-06_

GeoMaker is a generative-art studio that runs entirely on your device.

## The short version

**GeoMaker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**
There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.
The app makes no network requests — every pattern is generated on your device.

## Details

- **No data collection.** GeoMaker does not gather personal information, usage
  analytics, identifiers, or location. There is nothing to opt out of because
  nothing is collected.
- **No network access.** The app is fully self-contained and works offline. It
  does not contact any server, including ours.
- **Your settings stay on your device.** Your last session (chosen algorithm,
  seed, palette and slider values) is saved locally on the device using standard
  on-device storage so the app reopens where you left off. This never leaves the
  device.
- **Images you create stay yours.** When you tap Save, GeoMaker hands the image
  to the system share sheet so *you* can choose to save it to Photos or Files, or
  share it. GeoMaker does not upload or retain a copy. Saving to your photo
  library uses the standard iOS permission (`NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription`)
  and only happens when you choose it.
- **No tracking.** GeoMaker does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA), App
  Tracking Transparency, or any cross-app tracking.
- **Purchases are handled by Apple.** The optional one-time unlock is processed
  entirely by the App Store (StoreKit). GeoMaker never sees your payment
  details; it only receives Apple's yes/no answer about whether the unlock is
  owned, which is checked on your device.
- **Children.** GeoMaker is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone,
  including children.

## App Privacy label

In App Store Connect this app is declared as **Data Not Collected**.
